BUBBLE HELL

This game is not available on mobile devices! Please visit this page on a desktop or laptop computer. Thanks!

  • Use the arrow keys to control the white bubble.
  • Avoid the other bubbles! When you pop a bubble, you lose 1 HP
  • When your HP reaches 0, the game is over.
  • The longer you stay alive, the higher your score.
